About

I am very tech oriented and passionate about what I do, and have very good problem solving, trouble shooting, and creative thinking skills. I have been video editing for about 10 years, slowly picking it up from my father, who does it professionally. I am now very comfortable in Final Cut Pro, and familiar with Premier Pro. I have edited videos for multiple companies, other peoples projects, social media videos, internal corporate videos, home videos, and more. I have also worked IT for the past two years, learning about all ends of tech, encountering and trouble shooting hundreds of issues. I am also assisting a small company with creating a Linux distro, providing funding through video editing consultations, and piloting user testing and feedback. I have also done a lot of tech help to my local teen tech center, such as grant allocation, setup and maintenance of all the new tech, upgrading their laptops, and providing consultaion to many of their decisions. I also do a lot of volunteering at electronics recycling centers, and do my own tech projects very often, such as fixing old laptops, recovering data, building computers, pushing the limits of software, 3D modeling, graphic design, tech consulting, messing with servers and random computers, and collecting and cataloging old tech.

I also enjoy riding my bike, seeing the city, sitting around a bonfire, hanging out with people, and analyze media.
